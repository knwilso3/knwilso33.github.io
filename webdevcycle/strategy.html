What is strategy in the web-dev-cycle?

In the web development life cycle (WDLC), the strategy phase is a foundational step.
Strategy includes the project's overall direction, goals, and scope are defined to ensure the final product aligns with business objectives and user needs.
It acts as a roadmap for the entire development process, guiding all decisions in the next steps of the web-dev-cycle!.
