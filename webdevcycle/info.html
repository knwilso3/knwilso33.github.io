What is Information gathering?

Information gathering is one of the most important steps of the entire proccess!
Info gathering is identifying your audience, understanding your projects goals, your strategy for development, and understanding the websites use and needs.

How can I gather info?

You can host a discovery meeting with your client to learn your clients goals and what they may want their website to look like.
Then you can research the topic online on trusted websites you can find on google.
Then you will identify your audience and start on the next step of the Web-development-cycle!
