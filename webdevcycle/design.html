What is Design in the web-dev-cycle?

The Design Cycle involves four key steps:

  1. Planning: Setting goals and brainstorming initial ideas.
  2. Development: Shaping and refining those ideas.
  3. Creation: Bringing designs to life and refining them based on feedback.
  4. Evaluation: Checking outcomes against criteria, gathering user feedback, and making improvements.
